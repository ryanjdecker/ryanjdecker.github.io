NON FUI

BY ROOK

On their gravestones, the Epicureans used to write

Non fui
Fui
Non sum
Non curo

I was not
I was
I am not
I care not

Founded by the Greek philosopher Epicurus (341 to 270 BCE), this school of philosophy held that humans do not survive death.
