Observations on capitalism

In this note, I'm collecting data that inform my ethical judgments on capitalism. So, these are not necessarily original observations of my own, but observations that have been made by others. I generally prefer the term observation to data because I find it easier to remember the active role that humans play in making observations rather than merely collecting data--and it also helps me to remember that observation is theory-laden.

In 2017, Oxfam reported that "eight men own the same wealth as the poorest half of the world" (Hardoon 2017).
