NON FUI

BY RYAN J. DECKER

On their gravestones, the Epicureans used to write

Non fui
Fui
Non sum
Non curo

I was not
I was
I am not
I care not

Founded by the Greek philosopher Epicurus (341 to 270 BCE), this school of philosophy held that death is really the end for us humans. Since a human is just a physical process, there's nothing there to survive the end of that process.

The mind is a dance of the brain. When the brain dies, the dance ends.

The alternative to accepting the finality of death is to invent fairy tales.

Where does the idea that humans survive death come from?
